Privacy Policy – Учи и цртај – Букви и броеви

Last updated: December 2024

Учи и цртај – Букви и броеви is an educational mobile application designed for children to learn the Macedonian alphabet and numbers through drawing and sound.

Information Collection and Use

This application does not collect, store, share, or process any personal data.

No user accounts

No analytics

No advertisements

No location access

No internet communication

No third-party services that collect data

All content (images, sounds, and drawings) is processed locally on the device.

Children’s Privacy

This application is designed specifically for children.
We comply with Google Play’s Families Policy and COPPA requirements.

Changes to This Policy

If the app functionality changes, this policy will be updated accordingly.
